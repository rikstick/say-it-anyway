Say It Anyway

Powered by advanced algorithms, this app distorts your text to stay readable for humans — but tough for bots to decode..

What Is It?

Say It Anyway is an Android app that transforms your text using advanced techniques to make it difficult for bots to detect and moderate — while keeping it readable for humans. Whether you're a creator, a privacy advocate, or just someone who wants to express themselves more freely, this tool gives you control over your own words.

Who It's For

This app is designed for individuals who want to add an extra layer of creativity or privacy to their communication. It's especially helpful in environments where over-aggressive automated moderation can silence harmless speech.

Responsible Use

Please note: This app is not intended to be used in ways that violate the terms of service of any platform or website. It is a tool for personal expression and privacy, not evasion of legitimate rules or policies.
